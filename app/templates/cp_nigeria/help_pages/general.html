Welcome to the CP Nigeria Toolbox. This toolbox is aimed towards communities interested in approaching stakholders to initiate a mini-grid project. The Toolbox aims to empower communities by equipping them with all necessary tools and information to start reaching out to potential stakeholders.
The toolbox will guide you through a number of steps with the goal of
  1. Estimating the communities' future electricity demand
  2. Sizing a mini-grid to satisfy the estimated demand
  3. Supporting you in choosing a viable business model for the mini-grid
  4. Conducting a financial analysis and estimating a potential tariff for the mini-grid project
  5. Generating a comprehensive report that can be used to address project partners
