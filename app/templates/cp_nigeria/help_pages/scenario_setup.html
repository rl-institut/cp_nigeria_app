Energy System Layout

Once the community demand has been estimated, the supply system can be defined. To simplify usage to non-experts, the interface only provides options to select or un-select the following components: diesel generator, solar PV plant and battery storage. Default values

For each of the assets, you may expand and edit a number of technical and economic parameters. Hovering over the button provides a more detailed explanation for each parameter.

The toolbox provides default values for all parameters based on the current state of the art. The sources for the given default parameters include:

  • Mini Grids for Half a Billion People: Market Outlook and Handbook for Decision Makers, ESMAP - World Bank, 2022
  • Tariff Considerations for Micro-Grids in Sub-Saharan Africa, National Renewable Energy Laboratory, 2018
  • Minigrids in the Money: Six Ways to Reduce Minigrid Costs by 60% for Rural Electrification, Rocky Mountain Institute, 2018
  • Nigeria Diesel prices, litre, 04-Sep-2023 , GlobalPetrolPrices.com - with +50% markup for logistics and losses/theft, September 2022

PV Timeseries

If solar PV plant is selected, a timeseries representing the solar generation potential of the site is needed. In the toolbox, a timeseries is automatically downloaded from Renewables.ninja based on the project coordinates selected during step 1.
